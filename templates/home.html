About Us

About Cerculean

At Cerculean, we're a family-run apparel sourcing partner specialising in sustainable and regenerative solutions for UK and European brands. With 20+ years' expertise, we streamline global supply chains—managing ethical procurement, quality control, and on-time delivery from concept to consumer.

We source high-quality, ethically produced garments—including denim, jerseywear, polos, casualwear, dresses, and activewear—from certified mills and vertically integrated factories.

Our Services

Our end-to-end service covers fabric development, garment engineering, compliance, and in-line quality checks. We bridge creativity with technical precision, helping clients select eco-conscious materials like organic cotton, recycled polyester, TENCEL™️, and BCI-certified yarns.

Skilled in tech packs, MOQ management, and AQL standards, we act as your seamless extension—ensuring scalable, responsible production without compromising quality or innovation.
